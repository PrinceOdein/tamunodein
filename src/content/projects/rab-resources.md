## Overview

Rab Resources is a business website built to showcase services including
logistics, car hiring, CCTV installation, and solar solutions.

## Problem

The company needed an online presence that could clearly present its
services and be updated when new offerings or products became available.

## Solution

A responsive landing page was built with a simple backend system that
allows updates without modifying the frontend code.

## Key Features

- Service listings
- Simple content management
- Responsive design
